# Alternative Game Specs

## Core Principle
The game should be entertaining even if the token price is flat.

## Pot
- Players deposit ETH.
- Small protocol fee (1-3%) funds treasury.
- Remaining ETH enters prize pool.

## Win Flow
- Winners receive most of the pot.
- Small percentage remains to create rollover jackpots.
- Jackpot grows during losing streaks.

## Token
- Fixed maximum supply.
- Mint only from a launch allocation.
- No continuous emissions.

## Utility
- Governance.
- Reduced protocol fees.
- Staking for revenue share.
- Access to tournaments and special game modes.

## Treasury
Protocol fees accumulate in treasury.
Treasury funds development, audits, liquidity and marketing.

## Sustainability
Revenue comes from gameplay, not token inflation.
