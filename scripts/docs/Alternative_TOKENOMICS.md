# Alternative Tokenomics

## Philosophy
The token should represent ownership of the ecosystem rather than being used as the reward itself.

## Supply
Max supply: 100,000,000

40% Community
20% Treasury
15% Liquidity
15% Team (4 year vesting)
10% Strategic Reserve

## Revenue
Every game:
- 97% to prize pool
- 2% treasury
- 1% buyback

Buybacks purchase tokens from market.
Purchased tokens are:
- burned, or
- distributed to stakers.

## Inflation
No new token emissions after launch.

## Advantages
- Predictable supply
- ETH drives the economy
- Token value linked to protocol success
- Easier for investors to understand

## Risks
Lower early user incentives.
Requires enough game volume to sustain buybacks.
